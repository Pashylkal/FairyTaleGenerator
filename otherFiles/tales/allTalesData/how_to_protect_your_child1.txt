German MythologyPlacing a key next to an infant will prevent him from being exchanged.Women may never be left alone during the first six weeks followingchildbirth, for the devil then has more power over them.During the first six weeks following childbirth, mothers may not goto sleep until someone has come to watch the child. If mothers areovercome by sleep, changelings are often laid in the cradle. To preventthis one should lay a pair of men's pants over the cradle.Whenever the mother leaves the infant's room she should lay anarticle of the father's clothing on the child, so that it cannot beexchanged.
