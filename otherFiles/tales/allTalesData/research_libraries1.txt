Online catalogs of large libraries are excellent sources of bibliographicinformation about published folktale books. However, please note thatthese catalogs do not provide access to the books themselves. Thefollowing electronic catalogs are well designed and provide informationabout immense collections:COPAC , a unioncatalog combining the resources the largest university libraries in theUnited Kingdom and Ireland plus the British Library.Library of Congress. Of special interest at the Library of Congress is the FolklifeSourcebook .IndianaUniversity Libraries . One of the world's largest folklore collections.Melvyl , theonline catalog of the University of California Libraries.
