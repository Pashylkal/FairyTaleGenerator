We mustenter the castle with more ceremony. Wait for me here, and in an hour Iwill return with carriages and horses fit for a princess.''Don't be long,' replied Zizi, and she watched him go with wistful eyes.When she was left by herself the poor girl began to feel afraid. Shewas alone for the first time in her life, and in the middle of a thickforest.Suddenly she heard a noise among the trees. Fearing lest it should bea wolf, she hid herself in the hollow trunk of a willow tree which hungover the fountain. It was big enough to hold her altogether, but shepeeped out, and her pretty head was reflected in the clear water.Then there appeared, not a wolf, but a creature quite as wicked andquite as ugly.
