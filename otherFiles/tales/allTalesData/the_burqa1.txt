Nasreddin Hodja's first marriage was an arranged marriage, and inkeeping with the custom of the time, he did not see his unveiled brideuntil the wedding ceremony. Unfortunately, she did not have an attractiveface.The next day when the bride was making preparations to go to market,she asked her husband, as was the custom, "Shall I wear my burqa? I do notwish to show my face to anyone against your wishes."Nasreddin answered, "Wear your burqa or leave it at home. It is all thesame to me to whom you show your face in public. All I ask is that youkeep your face covered when you are at home with me."
