It is said that the Night Huntsman haunts the vicinity of the UdarserMill. Once a mill worker who had spent the night at the mill heard theNight Huntsman passing by with great commotion, shouting "Halloo!" Theworker had heard a lot about the Night Huntsman's sinister deeds, and hewanted to know more about him, so he went out onto the mill platform andheartily added his voice to the wild noise. Suddenly he heard a voicecalling out:If you want to hunt,You can join the ride!At the same time someone threw a woman's leg at the worker, a woman's legwearing a red shoe. The worker quickly retreated into the mill. It issaid that the next morning he buried the leg beneath the mill platform.
