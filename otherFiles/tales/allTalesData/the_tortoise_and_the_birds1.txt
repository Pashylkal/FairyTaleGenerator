A tortoise desired to change its place of residence, so he asked aneagle to carry him to his new home, promising her a rich reward for hertrouble. The eagle agreed, and seizing the tortoise by the shell with hertalons, soared aloft. On their way they met a crow, who said to the eagle,"Tortoise is good eating.""The shell is too hard," said the eagle in reply."The rocks will soon crack the shell," was the crow's answer. and theeagle, let fall the tortoise on a sharp rock, and the two birds made ahearty meal off the tortoise.Never soar aloft on an enemy's pinions.
