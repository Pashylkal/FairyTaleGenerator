A cunning old rat discovered in his rounds a most tempting piece ofcheese, which was placed in a trap. But being wellaware that if he touched it he would be caught, he slyly sought one of hisyoung friends, and, under the mask offriendship, informed him of the prize. "I cannot use it myself," said he,"for I have just made a hearty meal."The inexperienced youngster thanked him with gratitude for the news, andheedlessly sprang upon the tempting bait. onwhich the trap closed and instantly destroyed him. his companion, beingnow quite secure, quietly ate up the cheese.Moral: Do not listen to every passer by.
