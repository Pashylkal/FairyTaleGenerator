A married couple in Hessen lived in poverty. To the husband's amazement,thewife nevertheless was able to serve meat for every meal. For a long timeshekept it a secret where she got the meat, but finally she promised toreveal itto him, under the condition that he not call out her name as it washappening.Together they went to a field where a herd of sheep was grazing. Thewomanwalked toward the sheep, and as she approached them, she threw a ring overherself and instantly turned into a werewolf. She fell upon the sheep,seizedone of them, and fled. The man stood there as though petrified. However,whenhe saw the shepherd and the dogs running after the werewolf, thusendangeringhis wife, he forgot his promise and called out: "Margaret!" With that thewolfdisappeared, and the woman was left standing naked in the field.
