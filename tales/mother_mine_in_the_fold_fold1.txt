A young woman who lived on a farm became pregnant. After giving birthto the child she set it out to die of exposure, not an uncommon act inthis country before it became punishable by severe penalties. Now one dayit happened that the young woman was invited to a dancing party. However,she had no good clothes, so she stayed at home in a sour mood. Thatevening, while milking the ewes in the fold, she complained aloud that forthe want of a proper dress she could not go to the party. She hadscarcely spoken when she heard the following song:Mother mine, in the fold, foldYou need not be so sad, sad.You can wear my castoff rags,So you can dance,And dance.The young woman who had let her child die of exposure thought that sherecognized its voice. She took such a fright that she lost her mind andremained insane the rest of her life.
