Meanwhile the song was sunga third time. _Ta tai tom_ had left the lips of the singer. and,before _tadingana_ was out of them, the traders separated into partiesof three, and each party pounced upon a thief. The remaining one theleader himself tore up into long narrow strips a large piece ofcloth, six cubits long, and tied the hands and feet of the robbers.These were entirely humbled now, and rolled on the ground like threebags of rice!The ten traders now took back all their property, and armed themselveswith the swords and cudgels of their enemies. and when they reachedtheir village, they often amused their friends and relatives byrelating their adventure.
