In Caseburg on the island of Usedom a man and his wife were cutting hay inameadow. After a while the woman told the man that she was uneasy andcould notstay there any longer, and she went away. Earlier she had told him thatif awild animal were to come upon him he should throw his hat toward it andrunaway, and then no harm would come to him. The man had promised her thathewould do this.After the woman had been away for a while, a wolf swam across the Swinaandapproached the harvesters. The man threw his hat at it, which the beastimmediately ripped into small pieces. Meanwhile one of the workers creptup tothe wolf with a pitchfork and stabbed it to death from behind. Instantlyit wastransformed. They were all astounded to see that it was the farmer's wifethatthe worker had killed.
